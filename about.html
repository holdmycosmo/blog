About Me

My name is Veronika. I am a student and a researcher at a small university in central Europe. Get to know me better by reading about my education path and personal motivation for starting this blog.


About My Education

I have finished my physics Bachelor’s degree in 2016, and continued my studies to obtain a Master’s degree in elementary particle physics. I have done my Master’s thesis in the field of cosmology. More particularly, I used the output of large cosmological simulations to measure the power spectrum of the matter distribution in the Universe. I enjoyed the work a lot, so I decided to pursue further education in this field. After finishing my Master’s in September 2019, I enrolled into a Ph.D. program and started my research.

About The Blog

Motivation

The idea to start a blog has been in my mind for a very long time. In science, and especially for science students like me, it is extremely beneficial to not only study the material we are working on but also to immerse ourselves in discussions about the field with other students and researchers, as this is helping us understand the phenomena more in-depth, as well as enhancing the creative process of solving problems when working on your research. In addition to immersing ourselves deeper in research, writing a blog is also beneficial for gaining experience in writing and explaining physical concepts, which comes in handy when writing a thesis.
I have therefore decided that starting a blog will be a nice addition to my existing research endevour, as well as (hopefully) a useful resource for at least a handful of enthusiastic physics students out there.

Purpose

The main reason for starting a blog lays in my passion for physics and cosmology. I would like to connect with people who share the same interest and enjoy the process of creating educational and helpful content. The purpose of the posts posted on this blog and my activity on other social media platforms is to give people the motivation to study and learn intensively and enjoyably. My goal is to provide this help in the form of articles, in depth explanations, tutorials, Quizlet sets, and courses on physics and cosmology. I would like this blog to be a place for physics students to come for additional studying motivation, useful advice, and quality studying resources. I am looking forward to creating this type of content!

Side Projects

Most of the work I do is focused around my research, which consists of a lot of coding, reading the literature, and discussing the research projects with my coworkers. When I'm not working on my research, I am either writing and producing content you can find on this blog, or learning about and coding my personal machine learning projects.
